# Post-Release Experiment Checklist

Work through this after the product is live and has had some real contact with the market. The goal is to decide what to measure and what to try next, based on evidence rather than guesses.

- [ ] Log a Reality Report for every distinct piece of real user contact (using REALITY_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md).
- [ ] After a batch of contact/feedback, look for a pattern across reports, not just one data point.
- [ ] Decide: does this evidence confirm, weaken, or kill an assumption in DECISIONS.md?
- [ ] If two consecutive cycles produce no real external contact (no users, no feedback, no measurable evidence), stop and change the distribution approach rather than repeating it.
- [ ] Pick exactly one next experiment based on the strongest evidence so far — avoid running many untested changes at once.
- [ ] Record what was measured, what changed, and why in DECISIONS.md before starting the next cycle.
